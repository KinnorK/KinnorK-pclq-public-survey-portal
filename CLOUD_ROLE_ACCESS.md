# Role Access

## Public participant

- Opens `/survey` without login.
- Completes and submits the questionnaire.
- Receives the generated PCLQ ID.
- Cannot see any score, database record, report, dashboard, or export.

## Founder

- Full analysis dashboard.
- All survey data and item-level responses.
- State, date, time, background, literacy, and score filters.
- Individual reports and filtered exports.
- QR/public link administration.
- Founder and Analyzer account management.
- Audit log.
- Controlled record deletion.

## Analyzer

- Analysis dashboard.
- Submitted survey data and item-level responses.
- State, date, time, background, literacy, and score filters.
- Individual reports and filtered exports.
- No user management.
- No audit-log access.
- No QR administration.
- No permanent deletion.
